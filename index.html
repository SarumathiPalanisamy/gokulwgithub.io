IT INDUSTRY

  • The information technology profession is extremely diverse.
  • Information technology (IT) professionals help organisations maintain their digital infrastructure and provide troubleshooting assistance to technology consumers.
  • IT employees are in demand to help others keep up with technological advances and security procedures.
  • Many businesses seek IT professionals with mixed or overlapping skill sets.

IT workers can specialize in fields such as software development:

  1. application management
  2. hardware components
  3. server, storage or network administration
  4. network architecture and more
